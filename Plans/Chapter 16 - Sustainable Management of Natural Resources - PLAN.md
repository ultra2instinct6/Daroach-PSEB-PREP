## Slide-by-slide blueprint (30)
1. **Cover** — "Chapter 16 · Sustainable Management of Natural Resources" + Punjabi ਕੁਦਰਤੀ ਸਰੋਤਾਂ ਦਾ ਟਿਕਾਊ ਪ੍ਰਬੰਧਨ.
2. **Core Vocabulary** — sustainable development, stakeholder, watershed, biodiversity, dam, groundwater.
3. **Why Manage Natural Resources?** — limited resources + future generations.
4. **Sustainable Development** — meeting present needs without compromising future needs.
5. **The 5 R's** — Refuse, Reduce, Reuse, Repurpose, Recycle (with daily examples).
6. **INTERACTIVE — pd-game:** sort actions into the 5 R's.
7. **Forests and Wildlife** — biodiversity, ecological balance, livelihoods.
8. **Stakeholders in Forest Conservation** — local people, forest department, industrialists, wildlife enthusiasts.
9. **Chipko Movement** — community conservation case study.
10. **People's Participation** — why local communities must be involved.
11. **Dams: Benefits** — irrigation, electricity, flood control, drinking water.
12. **Dams: Problems** — social displacement, environmental damage, economic cost.
13. **Water Harvesting** — recharge groundwater; reduce runoff.
14. **Traditional Water Harvesting Systems** — khadin, tanks, johads, kulhs (regional examples).
15. **Watershed Management** — soil/water conservation, vegetation cover.
16. **INTERACTIVE — pd-game:** match water problem → solution (runoff→check dam, low groundwater→recharge pit, soil erosion→vegetation).
17. **Coal and Petroleum** — non-renewable fossil fuels; formation takes millions of years.
18. **Why Conserve Coal & Petroleum?** — finite supply, pollution, climate impact.
19. **Cleaner Choices** — public transport, efficient appliances, switching off, renewable energy.
20. **Energy, Economy and Equity** — resources must be shared fairly.
21. **Biodiversity Loss** — causes and effects.
22. **Conservation Strategies** — protected areas, sustainable harvest, afforestation, community management.
23. **Case Study: Local Resource Conflict** — water/forest use by villagers vs industry; balanced solution.
24. **Exam Writing Frame** — "How can an individual contribute to sustainable management?".
25. **Compare: Our Environment vs Sustainable Management** — ecosystem concepts vs human management actions.
26. **Spot the Error** — common misconceptions: "recycling is always first", "dams have only benefits", "forests only provide wood".
27. **Chapter Recap** — 10 one-line must-knows.
28. **True or False (8 Q)** — quiz sub-slider.
29. **Say It Back (8 Q)** — short answers with Anki metadata.
30. **Quick Check (8 MCQ) + Anki export slide** — compact final assessment/export.
