## Slide-by-slide blueprint (30)
1. **Cover** — "Chapter 14 · Sources of Energy" + Punjabi ਊਰਜਾ ਦੇ ਸਰੋਤ.
2. **Core Vocabulary** — fuel, calorific value, renewable, non-renewable, biomass, geothermal, tidal.
3. **What is a Good Source of Energy?** — high energy output, easy storage/transport, low cost, low pollution.
4. **Conventional vs Non-conventional Sources** — overview table.
5. **Fossil Fuels** — coal, petroleum, natural gas; how they formed; why finite.
6. **Thermal Power Plant** — energy conversion chain: chemical → heat → mechanical → electrical.
7. **Limitations of Fossil Fuels** — air pollution, greenhouse gases, acid rain, non-renewable.
8. **Improving Fuel Efficiency** — complete combustion, smokeless chulhas, better stoves.
9. **Hydro Power Plants** — dams convert potential energy to electricity.
10. **Environmental Problems of Dams** — displacement, submergence, ecosystem disruption.
11. **Biomass** — wood, crop residue, cow dung; why direct burning is inefficient.
12. **Biogas Plant** — construction + working; methane-rich gas; slurry as manure. *(diagram/SVG)*
13. **Wind Energy** — windmill farm; conditions required; advantages & limitations.
14. **INTERACTIVE — pd-game:** "Sort energy source: renewable / non-renewable".
15. **Solar Energy** — solar cooker, solar cell/panel; photovoltaic effect (simple).
16. **Solar Cooker** — black surface, glass cover, mirror reflector; limitations.
17. **Solar Cells** — uses in satellites, calculators, street lights; cost issues.
18. **Energy from the Sea** — tidal, wave, ocean thermal energy (OTEC).
19. **Geothermal Energy** — hot spots, steam turbines; India example if desired.
20. **Nuclear Energy** — fission of uranium; high output; radioactive waste risk.
21. **Hydrogen as a Fuel** — high calorific value, clean product (water), storage challenge.
22. **Comparing Energy Sources** — table: output, reliability, pollution, renewability.
23. **Local Decision Scenario** — choose best source for village / city / remote area.
24. **INTERACTIVE — pd-game:** "Match source → major limitation" (solar→weather, wind→site, nuclear→waste, fossil→pollution).
25. **Exam Writing Frame** — how to answer "What are the qualities of a good fuel/source?".
26. **Chapter Recap** — 8 one-line must-knows.
27. **Case Study** — choosing energy mix for Punjab/India: reliable + cleaner transition.
28. **True or False (8 Q)** — quiz sub-slider.
29. **Say It Back (8 Q)** — short answers with Anki metadata.
30. **Quick Check (8 MCQ) + Anki export slide** — if space tight, combine export after MCQ.
